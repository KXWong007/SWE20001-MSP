Welcome to FoodEdge Gourmet Catering!

With nearly 5 years of experience, FoodEdge Gourmet Catering has been dedicated to providing exceptional food and beverage catering services for a variety of events in Kuching. Our commitment to quality, safety, and health ensures that whether you're hosting a corporate function, seminar, wedding, or any other event, you can trust us to deliver outstanding service.



At FoodEdge, we understand the importance of staying ahead in the ever-evolving food catering industry. That's why we're excited to introduce our new Online Food and Beverage Catering Management System (FCMS). This innovative system is designed to streamline our business processes and enhance the overall customer experience.



With the FCMS, our management and operations teams can say goodbye to manual tasks and focus on what they do best – delivering top-notch catering services. Our goal is to provide fast responses to inquiries, better understand our clients' preferences through analytics, and ensure seamless coordination across all aspects of our business.



Our Online Food Catering System offers a range of functions to meet the needs of different users, including:

• Catering Management Module
• Food and Beverage Maintenance Module
• Customer Membership and Profile Module
• Payment Module
• Business Analytics Module



We understand the importance of data accuracy and system reliability, which is why the FCMS is built to professional standards. Users can expect a robust system that responds promptly to their requests, with minimal waiting time. Accessible via web browsers such as Firefox, Google Chrome, and Microsoft Edge, our system ensures convenience for all users. Plus, with password protection, you can trust that your information is secure.



At FoodEdge Gourmet Catering, we're committed to providing you with an exceptional catering experience. Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large-scale event, trust us to deliver delicious food and impeccable service every time.



Thank you for choosing FoodEdge Gourmet Catering – where quality and excellence meet.
